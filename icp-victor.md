## SECTION 3: THE CORE PROBLEM THEY WANT SOLVED

**Problem 1:** Foreign healthcare hiring requires navigating source-country and local regulatory requirements that hiring managers aren't well-versed in.

**Problem 2:** AI/Data Centre hiring managers often aren't current with market trends and skillsets — they search for capabilities that were relevant 3–5 years ago, which risks their company falling behind.

**Why they haven't solved it yet:**
They can search online themselves, but lack the time and bandwidth to truly understand current market trends. What they actually need is a consultant who knows the market well enough to advise them — which shortens the hiring timeline and sharpens their requirements from the start.

---

## SECTION 4: WHAT THEY WANT INSTEAD

**Tangible outcome:** A shortlist of qualified, pre-screened candidates delivered quickly, plus a successfully filled role with a 3-month replacement guarantee.

**Emotional outcome:** Relief from the manual, needle-in-a-haystack search, and confidence that the hire is right for the business — not just a body in the seat.

**Identity outcome:** A hiring manager who is seen — by their own leadership — as making sharp, market-informed decisions instead of reactive ones.

## SECTION 7: ICP PERSONA SUMMARY

The ideal client is an HR or hiring manager in Singapore — in healthcare or the fast-growing AI/Data Centre space — who has an open headcount and not enough time, network, or up-to-date market knowledge to fill it well.
