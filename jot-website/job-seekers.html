Candidate FAQ

Questions candidates ask us.

Do you charge candidates any fees?

No — never. Our fees are paid entirely by the hiring companies we work with. You will not be charged for a placement, an application, or an introduction at any point.

Will my details be kept confidential?

Yes. We share your information with an employer only after you have agreed to be put forward for a specific role. Your search stays private until you decide otherwise.

I am a foreign healthcare worker — can you help?

Yes. For healthcare roles we manage the source-country and local regulatory requirements, passes and applications, so the process is handled properly on both sides.

What happens after I submit my CV?

We keep your profile in mind as suitable roles come up. If there is a match, we will reach out to scope it with you before anything is shared with an employer — and we will be transparent about where you stand throughout.
