Why Employers Work With Us

Recruitment that reads the market, not just the resume.

Most agencies treat the hire as the endpoint. We treat it as one data point in a larger conversation about where your industry — and your competitors — are heading.

Market-informed, not transactional

We advise on current skillset demand before the search starts — particularly in AI and Data Centre, where hiring against a three-year-old job description means hiring for the past.

A candidate pool as fresh as today

Our database is proactively collected and refreshed daily. The candidates we present are active and reachable — not stale leads from a static file.

Regulatory work handled for you

For foreign healthcare workers, we manage the source-country and local regulatory requirements, passes and applications — removing an entire category of workload.

An extended arm of your team

Regular, proactive updates throughout the search — and honest flags if an outcome is not ideal, with suggestions to fine-tune the role or salary.

Our Process

From scoping to guarantee, in five steps.

Requirement scoping

A free consultation to understand the role, the purpose of the hire, and the challenges you have already faced.

AI-assisted screening

We screen our daily-updated database, surfacing candidates who are active and reachable right now.

Qualified shortlist

Only screened, qualified candidates reach your desk — with regular updates throughout.

Win-win negotiation

We balance your budget against the candidate's expectations, not just the closing of a deal.

3-month guarantee

If the candidate leaves within 3 months, we provide a replacement or service credits.

Fees & Guarantee

Transparent terms, agreed before the search begins.

Our fees are charged to hiring companies only, as a percentage of the placed candidate's annual salary. The rate depends on the role, and is agreed with you up front — never a surprise at offer stage.

3-month replacement guarantee

If a placed candidate does not stay at least three months, we provide a replacement headcount or service credits toward your next hire. The guarantee is included in every placement.
