How We Work

A structured search, from scoping to guarantee.

AI speeds up our screening. It does not replace the consultant's judgment — every shortlist reflects a considered read on fit, not just a keyword match.

Requirement scoping

A free consultation to understand the role, the purpose of the hire, and the challenges you have already faced in filling it.

AI-assisted screening

We screen our daily-updated database, using AI tools to surface candidates who are active and reachable right now.

Qualified shortlist

Only screened, qualified candidates reach your desk — with regular updates and honest flags if the search needs fine-tuning.

Win-win negotiation

We facilitate an outcome that balances your budget against the candidate's expectations — not just the closing of a deal.

3-month guarantee

If the candidate leaves within 3 months, we provide a replacement or service credits toward your next hire.
